Japanese Egg Salad

Description

Egg salad may very well be the premier summer sandwich. Japanese egg salad takes plain egg salad to the next level. Served on a piece of Hokkaido milk bread, this light, fluffy treat is the perfect on-the-go meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 dozen eggs
  • Kewpie mayonnaise
  • Yellow mustard
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Sesame oil
  • Paprika

Steps

  1. Bring large pot of water to a boil. Boil eggs for 9 minutes.
  2. Remove eggs and immediately chill
  3. Peel eggs
  4. Dice 10 eggs. Make sure there is discernable chunk--you do not want to dice the eggs too finely.
  5. In a large bowl, add eggs, kewpie mayo, and mustard. Mix until incorporated.
  6. Add salt and pepper to taste, along with a dash of paprika
  7. Add an eighth of a tsp of sesame oil, mix egg mixture again
  8. Cut last 2 eggs in half lengthwise
  9. Spoon egg salad onto bread, leaving space in the middle to place halved eggs face-down
  10. cut in half and eat!
