Macaroni n Cheese

Description

Macaroni and Cheese is an American classic. As a child, there was nothing I loved more than coming home to a heaping plate of macaroni and cheese after playing outside all day. Below is a recipe for the creamiest, cheesiest, most flavorful macaroni and cheese that will bring you right back to your childhood.

Ingedients

  • Elbow macaroni
  • Grey Poupon Mustard
  • 1 block white cheddar
  • 1 block orange cheddar
  • Milk
  • Butter
  • Flour
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Boil pot of salted water. Once boiling, cook pasta.
  2. In separate pot, melt 3 tbsp butter
  3. Add 3 tbsp flour to create roux
  4. Slowly stir in 1.5 cups milk
  5. Simmer until thickened
  6. Incrementally add shredded cheddar to thickened sauce
  7. Once cheese is melted, add 1 tbsp Grey Poupon
  8. Add Salt and Pepper to taste
  9. Combine cheese sauce and pasta
